# Types

> **Status:** DRAFT — Structure in place, details pending

Your Type is the noun in your character sentence—what you fundamentally are.

---

## The Four Types

| Type | Role | Primary Stats |
|------|------|---------------|
| **Warrior** | Combat, protection | Might, Agility |
| **Adept** | Powers, manipulation | Mind |
| **Explorer** | Skills, versatility | Agility, Mind |
| **Speaker** | Social, leadership | Persona |

---

## Warrior

> *You solve problems with force, precision, or both.*

**Base Pools:**
- Might: 14
- Agility: 12
- Mind: 8
- Persona: 8

**Core Ability: Combat Training**  
Add +1d6 to all attack and defense rolls.

**Starting Traits:**
- Weapon Proficiency (choose one)
- Physical Conditioning

## Adept

> *You touch forces others can't perceive.*

**Base Pools:**
- Might: 8
- Agility: 10
- Mind: 14
- Persona: 10

**Core Ability: Esoteric Talent**  
You can attempt "impossible" actions within your Focus domain. Others can't even try.

**Starting Traits:**
- Arcane Knowledge (or Tech, or Psionic)
- Focused Concentration

*(Full ability progression TBD)*

## Explorer

> *You go places others fear, find things others miss.*

**Base Pools:**
- Might: 10
- Agility: 14
- Mind: 10
- Persona: 8

**Core Ability: Versatile**  
Add +1d6 to any roll using an untrained skill.

**Starting Traits:**
- Survival (or Streetwise, or Navigation)
- Keen Senses

*(Full ability progression TBD)*

## Speaker

> *You shape people, not things.*

**Base Pools:**
- Might: 8
- Agility: 8
- Mind: 10
- Persona: 16

**Core Ability: Influence**  
Add +1d6 to all social rolls. Once per scene, grant an ally 1 IP by encouraging them.

**Starting Traits:**
- Persuasion (or Deception, or Leadership)
- Read People
