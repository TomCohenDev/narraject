# Descriptors

> **Status:** DRAFT — Examples provided, full list pending

Your Descriptor is the adjective in your character sentence—who you are as a person.

---

## What Descriptors Provide

Each Descriptor grants:
- **Stat Bonus:** +4 to one stat OR +2 to two stats
- **Trait:** One free trait reflecting the descriptor
- **Flaw:** A narrative weakness

---

## Sample Descriptors

### Tough
**Bonus:** +4 Might  
**Trait:** Endurance  
**Flaw:** Blunt — Social subtlety escapes you

### Clever
**Bonus:** +4 Mind  
**Trait:** Quick Thinking  
**Flaw:** Overconfident — You assume you're the smartest

### Charming
**Bonus:** +4 Persona  
**Trait:** First Impressions  
**Flaw:** Easily Distracted

### Swift
**Bonus:** +4 Agility  
**Trait:** Initiative  
**Flaw:** Impatient

### Haunted
**Bonus:** +2 Mind, +2 Persona  
**Trait:** Sense the Unseen  
**Flaw:** Nightmares

### Cautious
**Bonus:** +2 to any two stats  
**Trait:** Danger Sense  
**Flaw:** Hesitant
