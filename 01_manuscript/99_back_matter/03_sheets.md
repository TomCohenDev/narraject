# Character Sheet Reference

A reference for filling out your character sheet.

---

## Character Sentence

> **"I am a [Descriptor] [Package] who [Focus]."**

**Example:** _"I am a Haunted Muscle who Never Misses."_

---

## Stats

For each stat (Might, Agility, Mind, Persona):

- **Rating:** Your innate capability (1-5)
- **Reserve:** Current / Capacity
  - Capacity = (Rating × 2) + 5

**Example:**
- Might Rating: 3
- Might Reserve: 8 / 11

---

## Step Die

- **Focus:** Your signature specialty
- **Tier:** 1 (d8), 2 (d10), or 3 (d12)
- **Current Step Die:** d8 / d10 / d12

---

## Traits

List your Traits:

- **Basic Traits:** +1d6 when relevant
- **Advanced Traits:** +2d6 when relevant

**Example:**
- Expert Marksman (Advanced, +2d6)
- Physical Conditioning (Advanced, +2d6)
- Survival (Basic, +1d6)

---

## Gear

List your gear with Tags:

**Example:**
- Scoped Rifle [Ranged, Quality, Loud] (+2d6 to ranged attacks)
- Light Armor [Concealable] (Reduce Condition by 1 level)
- Lockpick Set [Concealable, Quality] (+2d6 to lockpicking)

---

## Conditions

Track your Conditions:

- ☐ Hurt (-1d6)
- ☐ Wounded (-2d6)
- ☐ Critical (-3d6)

Conditions stack. Multiple Conditions = multiple penalties.

---

## Injector Points

Track your IPs:

- Current: ___ / 5 (max)

Use tokens (poker chips, coins, beads) for visual tracking.

---

## Notes

Space for:
- Character background
- Goals and motivations
- Relationships
- Session notes

---

## Quick Reference

**Pool Formula:** Rating + Exertion + Traits + Gear + Situational + Step Die

**Mark:** Any die showing 5+

**Success:** Marks ≥ Difficulty

**Step Die Check:**
- Highest → Gain 1 IP
- Lowest → GM gains 1 IP
- Shows 1-2 → GM gets free complication

**The Golden Rule:** Success = Intent Happens. Period.
