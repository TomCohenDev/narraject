# Packages

Packages replace "classes." They're pre-built archetypes that give you starting stats, a core ability, and suggested Traits.

Choose one. Each fits on one page.

---

## The Muscle

> _You solve problems with force, precision, or both._

**Starting Ratings:**
- Might: 3
- Agility: 2
- Mind: 1
- Persona: 1

**Starting Reserves:** Full capacity (Might: 11, Agility: 9, Mind: 7, Persona: 7)

**Core Ability: Combat Training**  
Add +1d6 to all attack and defense rolls.

**Starting Traits:**
- Weapon Proficiency (choose one weapon type)
- Physical Conditioning (Advanced)

**Suggested Focus:** Never Misses, Never Backs Down, Moves Like Water, Strikes From Shadow

**Starting Gear:** One quality weapon, light armor, basic supplies

---

## The Face

> _You shape people, not things._

**Starting Ratings:**
- Might: 1
- Agility: 1
- Mind: 2
- Persona: 3

**Starting Reserves:** Full capacity (Might: 7, Agility: 7, Mind: 9, Persona: 11)

**Core Ability: Influence**  
Add +1d6 to all social rolls. Once per scene, grant an ally 1 IP by encouraging them.

**Starting Traits:**
- Persuasion (or Deception, or Leadership) (Advanced)
- Read People (Basic)

**Suggested Focus:** Speaks with Authority, Knows Too Much, Commands the Dead, Solves Mysteries

**Starting Gear:** Fine clothes, disguise kit, social tools

---

## The Tech

> _You touch forces others can't perceive._

**Starting Ratings:**
- Might: 1
- Agility: 2
- Mind: 3
- Persona: 1

**Starting Reserves:** Full capacity (Might: 7, Agility: 9, Mind: 11, Persona: 7)

**Core Ability: Esoteric Talent**  
You can attempt "impossible" actions within your Focus domain. Others can't even try.

**Starting Traits:**
- Arcane Knowledge (or Tech, or Psionic) (Advanced)
- Focused Concentration (Basic)

**Suggested Focus:** Bears a Halo of Fire, Builds Impossible Things, Commands the Dead, Knows Too Much

**Starting Gear:** Tools of your trade, basic supplies, one quality item

---

## The Scout

> _You go places others fear, find things others miss._

**Starting Ratings:**
- Might: 2
- Agility: 3
- Mind: 2
- Persona: 1

**Starting Reserves:** Full capacity (Might: 9, Agility: 11, Mind: 9, Persona: 7)

**Core Ability: Versatile**  
Add +1d6 to any roll using an untrained skill.

**Starting Traits:**
- Survival (or Streetwise, or Navigation) (Advanced)
- Keen Senses (Basic)

**Suggested Focus:** Never Misses, Moves Like Water, Solves Mysteries, Strikes From Shadow

**Starting Gear:** Survival kit, ranged weapon, light armor, tools

---

## Package Flavors

Want to mix and match? Work with your GM to create a hybrid Package. Start with one Package's stats, swap one Core Ability, or combine Traits from different Packages.

The goal is a character you want to play, not perfect balance.
