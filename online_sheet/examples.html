Playtest Tips

Rolling Dice

Pool = Rating + Effort + Traits + Gear + Aspects − Flaws. Count 5-6 on d6s as Marks. Meet or exceed Difficulty to succeed.

Using Injections

Spend 1 IP → Roll the Injector Die alone → 3+ creates an Aspect (+1d6 on 5-9, +2d6 on 10+) for future rolls.

Earning IPs

Accept GM Intrusions (+1 IP), play your Flaws well (+1 IP), or advance story goals (+1 IP). Start each session with 2 IPs.

Golden Rule

Success = Intent Happens. Period. Declare what you want, the GM sets difficulty based on ambition. If you succeed, you get exactly what you described.
