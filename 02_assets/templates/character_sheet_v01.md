## Quick Reference

**Building the Pool:**
- Stat Spend: 1 point = 1d6
- Basic Trait: +1d6
- Advanced Trait: +2d6
- Standard Gear: +1d6
- Quality Gear: +2d6
- Step Die: d8 / d10 / d12

**Hits:** 5+ on any die

**Step Die Results:**
- Highest → +1 IP to you
- Lowest → +1 IP to GM
- Shows 1-2 → GM free complication

**Spending IPs:**
- 1 IP: Reroll Step Die
- 2 IP: Add +1d6 after roll
- 3 IP: Partial success on failure
- 1-5 IP: Narrative Injection (see menu)
